Research with Unitary Fund

The Unitary Fund believes that research is an important driver in quantum open source. We focus not only on our own research interests, but also building tools to enable the research of others and supporting that research via our microgrant program. Check out some of the many research projects we've been involved in or supported below!

Unitary Labs research and collaborations

The Unitary Fund research GitHub repo has source code for a variety of our research projects.

  • Mitiq: A software package for error mitigation on noisy quantum computers, source code

  • Digital zero noise extrapolation for quantum error mitigation

  • Extending quantum probabilistic error cancellation by noise scaling

  • Pulse-level noisy quantum circuits with QuTiP

  • Pulser: An open-source package for the design of pulse sequences in programmable neutral-atom arrays

  • Exceptional Point and Cross-Relaxation Effect in a Hybrid Quantum System
