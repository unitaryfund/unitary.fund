unitaryHACK 2022: The Unitary Fund hackathon supporting quantum open source projects returns from June 3rd, 2022

 2022-05-24
Unitary Fund Team

Unitary Fund is glad to announce the 2022 edition of unitaryHACK, which will be hosted from June 3rd to June 17th, 2022.

UnitaryHACK contributions support existing quantum computing projects, pays people for their work, and helps build critical professional skills like working on OSS or quantum computing stack tools. We want this event to show folks what amazing projects are out there, and help you find new ways and people to help grow your projects. We have added to the webpage what you can expect before and during the event, as well as the rules for the event.
